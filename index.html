The Ethos

Born from a region
that builds things to last.

The Bajío has always been a place where craftsmanship matters. Where meticulous attention to detail is not a luxury but a tradition. Noche de Gloria carries that same independent spirit into the ring.

Every card is curated with the care of a small-batch craft. Every night is staged with the intention and heritage that defines this region. We pair elite competition with a level of presentation that honors the culture surrounding it.

For the fighters, a platform worthy of their discipline. For the audience, an occasion built with the pride of Guanajuato. For our partners, association with a brand rooted in Mexican craftsmanship and designed to endure.

The Promoter

From León to the world.

Boxing should belong to the people who build it.

Giovanni Cabrera was born in Seattle and raised between the United States and Mexico. His roots run deep in León, Guanajuato, where his grandfather worked as a shoemaker. The kind of craft and discipline that shaped everything that followed.

He won the Chicago Golden Gloves in 2013 before crossing to Mexico, where he became Municipal Champion of León, State Champion of Guanajuato, and a national bronze medalist. As a professional, he built a 22-2 record as a WBC lightweight contender, trained under Freddie Roach at Wild Card Boxing Club, and served as a sparring partner for Manny Pacquiao. He has fought on ESPN and Pay-Per-View cards against some of the sport's most recognized names.

For Noche de Gloria, Gio steps to the other side of the ropes, not on the card, but building it. It is the beginning of what he set out to create: a platform where Latino fighters and the communities that raised them have a seat at the table. World-class boxing, staged in the places that made it, controlled by the people who live it.

Giovanni "El Cabrón" Cabrera · Founder & Promoter, Four Kings Promotions
